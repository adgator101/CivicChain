# Problem Validation Backed by Data or Research

- Citizens complain actively with **300 complaints per day in Kathmandu alone** ([Annapurna Express](https://theannapurnaexpress.com/story/25939/)).

- Although **Hello Sarkar settles 90 per cent of complaints** ([The Himalayan Times](https://thehimalayantimes.com/kathmandu/hello-sarkar-settles-90-per-cent-complaints)), it lacks:
  - Real-time tracking for citizens
  - Transparency dashboard
  - AI routing to contractors
  - Analytics for government

- This creates **The Black Hole** where citizens report issues but paper records and manual workflows cause complaints to disappear with **zero visibility** into:
  - Who is responsible
  - What action is taken
  - When it will be resolved

- Government officials face the **Operational BlindSpot** due to:
  - Disconnected systems
  - Inconsistent records
  - Absolutely no data for resource allocation or priority mapping

- **The Result:**
  - Delayed action
  - Unaccountable bureaucracy
  - Declining public trust
  - A system where nobody knows what happened to their complaint and nobody is held responsible

| **Federal Innovation Grants** | Variable | Government grants |
| **NGO Program Sponsorships** | Variable | NGO partnerships |

## Customer Segments

- **Wards:** Miscellaneous budgets
- **Municipalities:** CAOs hold direct approval up to NPR 300k under Section 41
- **Citizens:** End users rather than buyers

## Cost Structure

- High B2G acquisition costs (travel and in-person meetings)
- SMS gateway fees
- Cloud and AI compute
- Field support staff

# Competitive Analysis

| Feature | Hello Sarkar | CivicChain |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| **Complaint Intake** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| **Real-Time Tracking & Transparency** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| **AI Classification with Root-Cause Detection** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| **Community Verification with Dispute Loop** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

**Summary:** Hello Sarkar provides complaint intake but lacks real-time tracking and transparency, AI classification with root-cause detection, and community verification with dispute loop. CivicChain delivers complaint intake **along with** all these features.
